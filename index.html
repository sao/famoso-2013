20 years of history

With more than twenty years of history in Kern County, Famoso Nut Company is owned and operated by local growers. At Famoso, we prioritize the grower, ensuring the production of high quality almond meats and inshell products and customer service that only local owners can provide. Food safety and careful handling in the plant accompany sound financial management and attractive grower payouts. Whether you are an Almond grower, an Almond buyer, or you just love the taste of fresh Almonds, Famoso Nut Company can deliver a world class level of service.

Quality

We take pride in knowing that the products we send out the door are of the highest quality and will satisfy the most discriminating tastes. We know the difference between average quality Almonds and the high-yield, high-quality Almonds that we produce. Utilizing our plant-wide computer system, we track our Almonds so as to deliver the right product for each customer's demands. You can trust that you will receive what you asked for and then some.

Safety

The backbone of our HACCP certification are our Standard Sanitary Operating Procedures (SSOP) and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). These guidelines allow us to understand and control our Famoso Nut Company production process to create valuable and safe products for our customers. To develop these plans and procedures we worked closely with top-quality industry experts. We consulted with the best to become the safe and secure plant that we are today. The process for achieving this certification was rigorous, and we work hard to stay in compliance so as to meet the needs of an ever-changing environment.

Growers

Our timely and accurate reporting combined with our high grower returns and reliable customer service is what sets us apart from our competitors. We are committed to providing highly accurate and detailed reporting to keep all of our growers as informed as possible. We also pride ourselves on being financially sound so that we can always pay out on time.
